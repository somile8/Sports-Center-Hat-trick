About us

"Let's make Novi Sad active again, together!"

From the foundation in 2012. Sport center Hat-trick quickly became the synonim for fun, sportsmanship, healthy rivarly and recreation. The main goal of our center is to bring high quality indoors courts and pitches to the local (and not just local) community at the fair price. Aside from renting sport courts, Sport center Hat-trick also rents space for many different occasions (celebrations, weedings, proms, conferences, etc.)

The center is located on 1200m2 on the outskirts of Novi Sad and is made of:

  • two basketball courts
  • two football pitches
  • two volleyball courts
  • two tennis courts
  • snack bar
  • four locker rooms
  • parking lot
